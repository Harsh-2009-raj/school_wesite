ABOUT THE SHANTI NIKETAN SCHOOL

Learning Begins With Us, Best school in Bidupur

Shanti Niketan School, Bidupur is a premier, child centred Co-ed English Medium Day School committed to providing quality education for all its students. Recognized as the best school in Bidupur, it is the First School in India that was established under the aegis of the prestigious Mount Litera Chain of Schools. It was founded in the year 2000, under the dynamic leadership of the Founder Director Mr Shankar Chaurasiya.
